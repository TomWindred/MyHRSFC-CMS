Welcome!

A welcome from your Student Council:

Welcome to the Student Council website! Our job is to provide a mix of representations, activities and services to the students of Hills Road Sixth Form College. We are not for profit, so any money we make through our activities is ploughed straight back into providing you with more and better services.

We look after all of the societies on campus. We are governed by our Constitution, which outlines how we operate and what we do. The Council is managed by a collective of 11 officers, who work together with form representatives on a regular basis. Meet the council here »

Please check this site regularly.
