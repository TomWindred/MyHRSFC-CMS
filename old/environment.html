Our Aims


The committee aims to improve and sustain the environment inside and outside of the college by focusing on some key issues.


Our main priority at the moment is reducing paper usage in the college. The repro-graphics department alone is estimated to use 7 million pages a year. That's in excess of £20,000 worth of paper!


Every classroom is meant to have a recycling bin, but in fact this is often not the case. By improving the college recycling scheme and allowing students to bring unwanted notes in to be recycled we can vastly reduce waste and cost.


To this end there will be more recycling bins around college and the blue dumpsters at the front of the college can be used by students to recycle paper. We will also introduce can crushers and recycling bins for materials other than paper if this scheme is successful.
