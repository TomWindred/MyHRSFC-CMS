"Hello and Welcome!" from your Council...


Hey lower 6th - welcome to Hills! The first few weeks can be a bit daunting so we've put together a handy section on the site to ensure the process is as stress-free as possible for everyone involved.

Hills Road is an energetic, exciting place with a limitless opportunities for you to engage in, so make sure that you make full use of the range of societies and clubs we've worked hard to provide in the coming year.

If you want more information about what we do please feel free to look through the rest of this website which is specifically designed to get the most important information to you in a quick manner.

If you've any questions at all please email your Chair, or the entire council. You can also contact us anonymously using our online contact form. We're ready on standby to reply to any queries you may have.

Thanks for reading and have fun in the year ahead!!

- Your Student Council,
