Freshers october 2014

Our annual Freshers event occurred on 9th October and featured College DJs along with our headliner Dr P. Over 800 students attended and enjoyed the neon theme.

Autumn Event - Papworth Trust Charity Event september 2014

On 19th September, we held a live music event on the College site to raise money for local charity The Papworth Trust. It was a relaxed evening with some fantastic performances from College bands (headliner Kermode Spirit), plus free ice cream and candyfloss for all attendees. We raised over £700 for The Papworth Trust and are looking into organising a similar event in the future.
